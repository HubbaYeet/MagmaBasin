Server Rules

  1. Respect Others
    • Treat all players with kindness and respect. Harassment, bullying, and discrimination will not be tolerated.
  2. No Griefing
    • Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without their permission. This includes stealing items from chests.
  3. No Cheating or Hacking
    • Use of mods, hacks, or exploits to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited. Play fair and keep the game enjoyable for everyone.
  4. Keep Chat Clean
    • Avoid using offensive language, spamming, or advertising other servers. Keep the chat friendly and appropriate for all ages.
  5. No PvP Without Consent
    • Player versus player combat is only allowed if both parties agree. Do not attack or kill other players without their permission.
  6. Build Responsibly
    • Do not build too close to other players without their consent. Respect their space and avoid causing unnecessary lag with overly complex redstone contraptions.
  7. Report Issues
    • If you encounter any issues, bugs, or rule violations, report them to the server staff. Help us maintain a positive and fair environment.
  8. Follow Staff Instructions
    • Respect and follow the instructions given by server staff. They are there to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Consequences

  • Warning: Minor infractions may result in a warning.
  • Temporary Ban: Repeated or serious offenses may result in a temporary ban.
  • Permanent Ban: Continued or severe violations will result in a permanent ban from the server.
